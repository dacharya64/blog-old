So Many Releases, So Little Time

28/Jul 2016

It’s been a while since my last update—time spent redesigning my website, making a Hugo blog, and of course working on games! And after some hard work, I’m proud to announce the release of two of those games today!

FUSION GALAXY

MassDiGI’s Summer Innovation Program was where I got my first real start in developing games with a team, and after a summer of work in SIP and another summer of polish, I’m proud to announce the release of Fusion Galaxy—a fast-paced mobile game. Travel around the universe to collect elements, craft them together, and try to unlock the secret to creating life itself!

Get it on Google Play

ETERNAL FLOPNATION

After SIP, what was there to do but make more games? Over the last few months I worked with a few other SIP alums to finish up a game they were making, which has just been released for mobile. In Eternal Flopnation, try to flop your way to the top of the ocean while avoiding dangerous obstacles like crabs and birds. Hope you have as much fun playing as I did working on this!
